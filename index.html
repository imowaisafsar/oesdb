OUR STORY

Orient Energy Systems

Orient Energy Systems is one of the fastest grown group of multinational company, specialized in power generation and utility relevant industrial engineering products supply, services & solutions for captive power, steam as well as IPP power project. Conceived by a group of highly ambitious technical professionals in 1996, the company has risen phenomenally from a localized anonymous entity to one of the most dynamic & revered company not only in Pakistan but also in UAE & Bangladesh with a workforce of more than 2000.

Orient Energy Systems Ltd., Bangladesh

Orient Energy Systems Ltd., Bangladesh established in 2005 as a branch office of Orient Energy Systems FZCO. Since 2005, Orient Energy Systems Ltd., Bangladesh was operating their business as a Sole Authorized Distributor & Service Providers of GE Jenbacher Gas Engine Genset, Austria (Now formally well-known INNIO Jenbacher, Austria) till July, 2013. In that period we had installed 470 units of GE Jenbacher Gas Engine Genset in Bangladesh which total capacity was 871 MW.

After a shift in a business, Since 2017, Orient Energy Systems Ltd., Bangladesh represents World’s renowned brand products likes: Hyundai HFO & DF Engine Based EPC Turnkey Power Plant Project, Packaged Diesel (Perkins, Cummins, MTU, Volvo etc.) Engine Genset, HCTC Steam Turbine EPC Turnkey Project, Exhaust Gas Boiler (EGB), VAM, Air Compressor, Solar EPC Turnkey Based for Industrial & IPP Power Project in Bangladesh.
